How to use Pixel Pool

Pixel Pool helps you remove color redundancies in your pixel art. Often you'll find that when you intend for two (or more) colors to be the same, they're instead off by a few RGB points. Pixel Pool helps you find pixels that are visually identical in color and combine them to the exact same value.

It can also be useful in cases where you're using a wide variation of colors and want to combine and simplify.

Getting Started

After you upload your image, Pixel Pool will run through every pixel and displays all the colors it finds as swatches. To get started, select at least two swatches. The first swatch you select will be the source -- any swatch you select after will be merged into the source color.

If you'd like to see your selected colors side by side, open the comparison panel (you can also use the "c" key as a shortcut).

It can also be helpful to preview the change and toggle between the current image and proposed change. To do so, you can enter toggle mode (you can also use the "t" key as a shortcut).
